# Problem Statement

The rapid growth of digital payment systems has made financial transactions more convenient for many people. However, a significant number of individuals, particularly those in rural communities or areas with limited internet connectivity, are unable to access these services because they depend on smartphones and stable internet connections.

Additionally, many existing payment platforms do not provide a unified solution that combines internet-based transactions with USSD functionality. This creates a barrier for users who rely on basic mobile phones, limiting their ability to participate in the digital economy.

There is a need for a secure, reliable, and accessible payment platform that enables users to send and receive money regardless of the type of mobile device they own or the availability of internet connectivity.

PayLink addresses this challenge by providing a digital payment platform that supports both internet-based transactions and USSD services. By integrating trusted Mobile Money APIs and USSD technology, the platform promotes financial inclusion while ensuring secure, fast, and convenient financial transactions for all users.
